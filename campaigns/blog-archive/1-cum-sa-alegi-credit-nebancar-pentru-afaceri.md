## Content summary

Addresses Moldovan entrepreneurs' financing challenges. Explains non-bank lenders offer rapid approval (24-48h vs banks' 2-4 weeks). Annual interest rates 12-48%.

Key guidance: calculate exact funding needs, add 10-15% contingency, analyze 12-month cash flow, verify creditor transparency. "Principala diferență față de bănci constă în viteză."

Documentation: registration certificates, financial statements, bank extracts, income proof, administrator ID.

Common mistakes: fixating on interest rates while ignoring hidden fees, underestimating repayment capacity, using unlicensed lenders.
