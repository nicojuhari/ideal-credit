## Content summary
Contrasts myths vs realities for ID-only loans.

Myth 1: Not everyone qualifies — need stable income (verified 3-6 months), positive credit history, age 25-55, permanent employment.
Myth 2: Approval still takes hours to days — involves identity verification, payment capacity evaluation, database checks.
Myth 3: Hidden fees do exist — opening fees, monthly admin charges, payment processing costs. Late penalties 0.1-0.5% daily. DAE reveals true costs.

Real advantages: simplified process, no collateral, faster than banks.
Real limitations: smaller amounts, shorter terms, higher costs due to perceived risk.
