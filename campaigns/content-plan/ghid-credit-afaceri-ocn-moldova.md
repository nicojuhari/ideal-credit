## Recommended Outline

**H1:** Cum obții credit pentru afacere la un OCN în Moldova: ghid pas cu pas

**Intro (150–200 words):**
Open with the entrepreneur's reality - they need capital to grow, buy stock, buy equipment, or stabilize cash flow. Banks feel complicated or say no. This guide shows exactly how the process works at an OCN (non-bank credit organization), specifically for business owners in Moldova.

**H2: Ce este un OCN și de ce îl aleg antreprenorii moldoveni?**

- Plain-language explanation of what an OCN is (vs bancă)
- Why OCNs exist and what they do differently - faster decisions, human judgment, no requirement for years of banking history
- Who can apply (SRL, II, persoană fizică cu activitate comercială)
- Mention that OCNs are regulated by CNPF

**H2: Pasul 1 - Calculează suma de care ai nevoie**

- Encourage honesty with self: what is the money for? (stoc, utilaje, extindere, capital circulant)
- How to estimate the right amount - not too little, not over-leveraged
- Anchor: mention the free calculator on idealcredit.md
- Include example: "Dacă ai nevoie de 80,000 MDL pentru utilaje noi și plătești 4% dobândă lunară, rata ta va fi X MDL pe lună..."

**H2: Pasul 2 - Pregătește dosarul**

- Plain list of documents typically required (see also dedicated documente article - internal link)
- Key point: at an OCN like Ideal Credit, the list is shorter and more focused than at a bank
- Mention what makes the biggest difference: business activity proof, not collateral perfection
- Common mistake: waiting until documents are "perfect" - apply and get guidance

**H2: Pasul 3 - Depune cererea**

- Explain the online pre-application (cerere online la idealcredit.md)
- What happens after: a call or email from the team to discuss next steps
- Realistic timeline: typically 1–2 business days for a decision with a complete file

**H2: Pasul 4 - Discuția cu echipa**

- This is what's different at an OCN vs online-only lender: there's a real conversation
- The team at Ideal Credit evaluates the business as a whole, not just credit score
- What the team looks at: activity, cash flow potential, purpose of the credit
- A human assessment, not an algorithm

**H2: Pasul 5 - Semnarea contractului și primirea creditului**

- What to read carefully: DAE, rata lunară, penalități anticipate
- Disbursement timeline after signing
- What happens next: monthly repayments, support from the team if anything changes

**H2: Întrebări frecvente despre creditul de afaceri la OCN**
Map PAA questions here as individual Q&A blocks:

- Ce documente sunt necesare? (link to dedicated article)
- Cât durează aprobarea?
- Pot aplica dacă am o afacere nouă?
- Pot lua credit fără gaj?
- Care este dobânda?

**H2: Unde ne găsești?**

- Căușeni (office address + map)
- Chișinău (office address + map)
- Online: cerere online + contact

**Closing CTA:**
Short, warm. "Dacă ți-ai calculat suma și ești gata să faci primul pas, depune cererea online - echipa noastră te contactează în aceeași zi."
